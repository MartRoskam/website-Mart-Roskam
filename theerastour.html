The Eras Tour

The Eras Tour is her ongoing sixth concert tour, Taylor described it as a journey through all of her musical "eras". An homage to her albums, the Eras Tour is her most expansive tour yet, with 146 shows across five continents.The show spans over three hours, with a set list of 44 songs divided into 10 distinct acts that portray the albums conceptually.
